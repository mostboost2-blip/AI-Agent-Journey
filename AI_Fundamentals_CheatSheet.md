# AI Fundamentals Cheat Sheet

## LLM
A model trained on large amounts of text to generate and understand language.

## Token
A small piece of text processed by the model.

## Context Window
The amount of information the model can remember in one conversation.

## Embedding
A numerical representation of text used for similarity search.

## RAG
Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Retrieve information before generating an answer.

## Agent
An AI system that can use tools and take actions to complete tasks.
